### 1. Plank
<ul>
    <li>Set: 3</li>
    <li>Duration: 30-60 seconds per set</li>
    <li>Rest: 30 seconds</li>
</ul>


### 2. Bicycle Crunches
- **Sets**: 3
- **Reps**: 15-20 per side
- **Rest**: 30 seconds

### 3. Leg Raises
- **Sets**: 3
- **Reps**: 12-15
- **Rest**: 30 seconds

### 4. Russian Twists
- **Sets**: 3
- **Reps**: 20 (10 per side)
- **Rest**: 30 seconds

### 5. Mountain Climbers
- **Sets**: 3
- **Reps**: 20-30
- **Rest**: 30 seconds

### 6. Flutter Kicks
- **Sets**: 3
- **Duration**: 30-45 seconds per set
- **Rest**: 30 seconds

## Notes:
- Make sure to warm up for 5-10 minutes before starting.
- Focus on maintaining proper form to avoid injury.
- Incorporate this workout 3-4 times a week for optimal results.
